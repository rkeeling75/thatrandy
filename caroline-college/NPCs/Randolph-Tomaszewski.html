    Randolph Tomaszewski

    Role/Title:

    • Member of Church of the Delayed Blossom

    Brief Description:

    Randolph is a tall, gaunt young man in his early twenties with a mop of dark hair and haunted eyes. His once-handsome face is now marred by deep-set scars, making him appear older than his years. He moves with an almost jerky motions, as if he's not entirely in control of his body.

    Biography:

    • Background: Randolph was born to a Polish immigrant family who moved to Watashuka when he was a child. Despite facing hardships growing up, he managed to excel academically and was on track to become a doctor before falling under the influence of the Church of the Delayed Blossom.
    • Personality: Randolph is introverted, often appearing withdrawn or disoriented due to his medication. He can be charming when he wants to be but also has a tendency towards volatility and aggression, especially when stressed or provoked. His once-sharp mind now seems clouded, and he struggles with memory and focus.
    • Motivations: Randolph's primary goal is to find a way to free himself from the Church of the Delayed Blossom's control. He wants to regain his independence and protect others from the cult's influence. Additionally, he seeks insight into his condition and a means to strike back against those who have wronged him.
    • Secrets: Randolph has been experimenting with necromancy, attempting to create a zombie that will obey his commands. He believes this creature will help him fight against the Church of the Delayed Blossom and uncover the truth behind his transformation into a ghast.

    Relationships:

    • Sgt. Jan Ducochik: Randolph has had some limited interactions with Sgt. Ducochik, who has shown him kindness and concern despite not knowing about his involvement in the cult. This interaction has left a positive impression on Randolph, making him more likely to trust or confide in the police officer.
    • Ronnie 'Moonglow' Regan: Ronnie's curiosity and enthusiasm for the occult have caught Randolph's attention. He sees her as a potential ally but also worries that her youth and thirst for the truth may turn her into a liability.
    • Leonid "Lenny" Kuznetsov: Randolph met Leonid Kuznetsov while Randolph was stealing food and supplies from the store, Dominick's, where Lenny works. Randolph recognized something about Lenny and began to take him under his wing. Within weeks, Lenny was recruited into the cult. Lenny acts as both Randolph's apprentice and servant.

    Hobbies (or rather, obsessions):

    • Necromancy: Randolph is fixated on raising the dead and harnessing their power to further his goals. He spends much of his free time researching ancient rituals and experimenting with corpse reanimation.
    • Underground Exploration: Randolph has developed an interest in exploring the extensive tunnel system beneath Watashuka, inspired by his escape from the medical facility where he was being held.

    Fears:

    • Fear of Manipulation: Randolph is terrified of being controlled or manipulated by others, especially those within the Church of the Delayed Blossom. This fear drives much of his behavior and motivates him to find a way to break free from their influence.
    • Fear of Failure: Randolph fears that he will not be able to complete his plans against the cult or find a way to reverse his transformation into a ghast. This fear often manifests as self-doubt or indecision.

    Goals:

    • Freedom from the Church of the Delayed Blossom: Randolph's ultimate goal is to sever his connection with the cult and gain control over his own life once again.
    • Uncovering the Truth: He wants to discover what caused his transformation into a ghast and find a way to reverse or manage it, ultimately using this knowledge to strike back against those who have harmed him.
