    Caroline College Overview

    Originally founded as Prairieville Academy, then Prairieville School, the college is a major economic driver for Watashuka. The college, and its forerunners, has been a focal point of cult activity since the 1850s. It is Despite its seemingly benign exterior, much of the cult’s influence stems from its leadership and long history with the school. The campus is also home to several factions including:
