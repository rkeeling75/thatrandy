    Mothers Opposing Metal (MOM)

    • Overview:
      Mothers Opposing Metal (MOM) is a highly vocal, conservative group based in Watashuka, comprised of concerned mothers, fathers, and other community members who believe that heavy metal music, role-playing games, and youth counterculture are gateways to devil worship and societal decay. Founded in response to the growing popularity of metal music and games like Dungeons & Dragons, MOM seeks to protect the townâs youth from what they perceive as occult influences, primarily targeting the local Metalheads and their culture.

      MOM has a strong influence over local politics, religious organizations, and the school system. Their efforts are centered on pressuring the Lutheran Church, the school board, and law enforcement to crack down on what they see as "satanic" influences in the town. Their public demonstrations, prayer vigils, and moral crusades often succeed in getting certain music, books, and games banned from schools and public spaces.

    • Base of Operations:
      MOM holds regular meetings at All Saints Lutheran Church, where its members gather for prayer, discussion of moral values, and the planning of public demonstrations. They also meet in the living rooms of prominent members for smaller organizing sessions. These gatherings are focused on protecting the youth and ridding the town of what they see as satanic influences.
