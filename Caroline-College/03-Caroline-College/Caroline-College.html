    Caroline College

    Welcome to Caroline College: Rooted in Tradition, Driven by Innovation

    Founded in 1856 as Prairieville Academy, Caroline College has been at the heart of Wisconsin's frontier history for over a century. Located in the picturesque town of Watashuka, nestled between rolling hills and just a short drive from Lake Geneva, our campus offers the perfect blend of historic charm and cutting-edge facilities. We honor our heritage while embracing the future—just as the founders of Prairieville Academy envisioned.

    Go Prairie Dogs! At Caroline College, we take great pride in our mascot, the Prairie Dog. Just like our tenacious mascot, our students are known for their resilience, teamwork, and school spirit. Whether you're cheering on our teams at Prairie Dog Stadium or collaborating with classmates on a project, you'll feel the energy and camaraderie that define Caroline College.

    Academic Excellence Meets Technological Advancement While we celebrate our rich frontier past, Caroline College is home to modern, world-class facilities. Our Qubit Tower, housing two of the most powerful supercomputers in the Midwest, stands as a testament to our commitment to cutting-edge research and technological innovation. In addition, our Brown Science Complex, including the Castelaz Building and Observatory and the Luttermoser Science Hall, equips students in the physical, biological, and chemical sciences with the tools they need to thrive in their fields. Whether you're stargazing at the Wright Telescope or conducting hands-on experiments, Caroline College is a hub for discovery.

    Spiritual Legacy Though no longer a religious institution, Caroline College remains deeply tied to its spiritual roots. The school’s founding as a religious teaching academy continues to shape our values and traditions. Our campus reflects this legacy, fostering an atmosphere of intellectual curiosity and ethical reflection, grounded in our shared history.

    A Scenic and Connected Campus Our idyllic campus combines serene, tree-lined walkways with modern conveniences, offering students the best of both worlds. Located just 100 miles from Chicago and 60 miles from Milwaukee, Caroline College provides access to vibrant city life while offering a peaceful, focused environment for learning and personal growth.

    Join Us at Caroline College At Caroline College, tradition meets innovation. We invite you to become part of a proud legacy that began with Prairieville Academy and continues today with unmatched academic opportunities, a vibrant campus life, and a commitment to student success.

    Explore Caroline College, where you’ll find your place in history and your path to the future. Go Prairie Dogs!
